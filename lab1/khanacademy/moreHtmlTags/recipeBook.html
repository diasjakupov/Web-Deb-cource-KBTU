Beshbarmak

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 800g
Meat (horse) 1.3kg
Spices ?g

Steps: Beshbarmak, a traditional Kazakh dish, is simple to make. Boil chunks of meat (lamb or beef) until tender, then cook broad, flat noodles in the same broth. Finally, layer the noodles on a serving dish, top with the meat, and spoon some broth over it, garnishing with finely chopped onions.

Pasta

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 800g
Meat (chicker) 1kg
Souce 100g
Spices ?g

Steps: Pasta is easy and quick to cook. First, boil the pasta in salted water until it's al dente, then drain it. Toss the pasta with your choice of sauce, such as tomato, alfredo, or pesto, and serve hot, optionally topped with grated cheese or fresh herbs.

Pizza

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 300g
Meat (Chicker) 300g
Spices ?g
Tomatos 100g

Steps:Making pizza is straightforward and fun. Start by spreading your choice of pizza dough on a pan, then add tomato sauce, your favorite toppings (like cheese, pepperoni, vegetables), and seasonings. Bake it in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for about 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
